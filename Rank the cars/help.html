Having trouble?

  • I cannot choose any option:

    • Sorry for trouble, we have following solutions for this problem:

    1. Try clearing cache memory of your browser.
    2. Try quitting and restarting your browser.
    3. Try restarting your computer.

    If this problem continues please contact us on the main website, and we will help you out of this situation.

  • Trouble with image loading:

    • The images that are being uploaded on the screen are directly sent through servers, and might give you trouble if your internet connection is not stable. But don't worry we have solution for this also!

    1. Please check your internet connection.
    2. Try connecting to a different network.

    If the problem with your internet still continues, please contact your internet service provider.

  • Found a bug!

    • Incase you found a bug, please let us know, we constantly try to make our services better and better.
    Please drop us a message if you found a bug: Found a bug!
